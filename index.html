Hello! I have had a variety of experiences within the tech world and am looking for my niche. Although I wasn't able to complete my degree, it is a field I am still highly interested in. I have been a TKH Fellow in the past. I completed the Digital Marketing Fellowship in January 2020. I was a 2020 Innovation Fellow for the Web Development Track, but ultimately decided it wasn't where I wanted to end up, so I left. However, the 2020 Innovation Fellowship did help me realize that I really enjoyed desigining wireframes for web dev projects we worked on. In the past I have had positions in marketing, software development, teaching, and even entrepreneurship. I believe the 2021 Innovation Fellowship will help me to find my place in the art-tech-design world and excel in doing so. This fellowship is ideal for me because of all the benefits being offered. TKH in the past has provided me with mentors, volunteers, mental health support and so much more. Although the fellowship might be rigorous, I have no doubt that they can mold me into one of the best UI/UX Designers. Not only that but, I hope that I can eventually give back to TKH staff and students as well.
